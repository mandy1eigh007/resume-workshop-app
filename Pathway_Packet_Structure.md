# Pathway Packet Structure

## Purpose

The Instructor Pathway Packet combines trade-specific content from the Stand-Out Playbook with append-only sections that track student progress, artifacts, and next steps.

## Base Content (Existing — Keep Intact)

The trade-specific section from `Stand_Out_Playbook_Master.docx` for the student's chosen trade remains unchanged.

## Appended Sections (NEW)

The following sections are added after the existing trade content:

### 1. Additional Credentials & Micro-Badges

* Trade-specific micro-badges from the Credential_MicroBadges_Library
* Each entry includes resume phrase and required proof artifact
* Students attach completion records or signed logs

### 2. Entry Tests & Physicals

* Cross-trade appendix content relevant to the student's trade
* Practice resource recommendations
* Verification requirements

### 3. BFET & Direct-Entry College On-Ramps

* Community college program calendars
* Manufacturer academies with public schedules
* Public utility trainee programs (where applicable)
* Open house/orientation schedules

### 4. Mobility Plan — Multi-State Application Rotation

* State apprenticeship portals
* Public job boards with calendar views
* Reputable industry association calendars
* Veteran pipelines
* Public utility careers pages (for linework)

**Each source includes:**
* Source name
* URL
* Refresh cadence
* Notes

### 5. Social Intel — Practical Insights

* Trade-tagged insights
* Links to resources
* Date added
* 1–2 line insight
* "How to evidence" guidance
* Status tracking

### 6. Checklist Update / Sources

* Week 0–2 checklist (Quick Wins)
* Weeks 2–6 checklist (Credential + Practice)
* Weeks 6–12 checklist (Portfolio + Applications)
