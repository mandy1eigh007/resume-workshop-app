========================================
FORMATTING GUIDELINES
========================================

Font: Arial, Calibri, or similar sans-serif, 10–11 pt
Margins: 0.5"–1.0" all sides
Length: One page maximum
Spacing: Single or 1.15 line spacing
Alignment: Left-aligned for body, name can be centered

Headers: Bold, slightly larger (12 pt)
Bullets: Use • symbol
Dates: MM/YYYY format or "Present"

========================================
CONTENT RULES
========================================

Objective:
- One line, neutral wording
- No sub-trade labels
- No union/non-union references

Experience:
- ≤3 jobs listed
- 3–4 bullets per job
- Each bullet ≤24 words
- Include quantities, measurements, or tools

Skills:
- ≤12 total skills
- Organized into 3 categories
- Use skills from Skills Canon

Certifications:
- Use normalized names only
- No expiration dates on resume (track separately)
- List most relevant first

Education:
- ≤2 entries
- Keep brief
- Include credential type and year

========================================
WHAT TO AVOID
========================================

× Adjective-only bullets ("hard worker")
× Invented employers or dates
× Sub-trade labels in objectives
× Union/non-union wording
× More than 12 skills
× More than 3 jobs
× More than 1 page
