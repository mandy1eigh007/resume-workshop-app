# Pathway Packet Checklists — Blank Templates

**Purpose:** Time-blocked action checklists for students to track progress toward apprenticeship or job entry.

**Usage:** Copy the relevant checklist into the Pathway Packet for the student's target trade. Students check off items as they complete them.

---

**Goal:** Stack low/no-cost proof artifacts fast to demonstrate safety habits, pace, and tool control.

## Ongoing Tasks (All Phases)

### Artifact Tracking
- [ ] Update Artifact Tracker after each new item
- [ ] Organize digital copies in labeled folders
- [ ] Note expiration dates for time-limited credentials
- [ ] Keep backup copies of all files

### Application Follow-Up
- [ ] Check application status every 2 weeks
- [ ] Respond promptly to requests for additional information
- [ ] Prepare for interviews (practice answers, gather documents)
- [ ] Send thank-you notes after interviews

### Skill Building
- [ ] Continue practice drills to improve metrics
- [ ] Seek feedback from instructors or supervisors
- [ ] Document improvements in logs
- [ ] Identify and work on weak areas
