# Prep Metrics Library

**Purpose:** Named drills and how they're recorded.

**Usage:** These are practice drills that students can perform to build evidence of capabilities. Each drill specifies what to record and how verification is documented.

---

## Timed Carry (2×50 lb, 50 ft)

**What to record:**
* Time (mm:ss)
* Path clear (Y/N)
* PPE used (list)
* Spotter (Y/N)
* Date
* Verifier initials

**Purpose:** Demonstrates safe material handling, pace, and use of proper body mechanics.

---

## Conduit Bending (three offsets, 10°/22.5°/45°)

**What to record:**
* Angles measured (±°)
* Take-up recorded
* Rework count
* Date
* Verifier initials

**Purpose:** Shows precision in electrical conduit work and ability to follow measurements.

---

## Device Terminations (10 devices)

**What to record:**
* Torque verified (Y/N)
* Continuity pass/fail count
* Time per device
* Date
* Verifier

**Purpose:** Demonstrates electrical termination skills and attention to proper torque settings.

---

## Ladder Setup (24 ft)

**What to record:**
* 4:1 rule verified (Y/N)
* Footing check (Y/N)
* Tie-off (Y/N)
* Inspector initials
* Date

**Purpose:** Shows understanding of ladder safety fundamentals.

---

## Leak/Pressure (training rig)

**What to record:**
* Pressure target (psi)
* Duration (minutes)
* Pass/fail
* Gauge photo (no faces)
* Date
* Verifier initials

**Purpose:** Demonstrates ability to conduct pressure tests and document results.

---

## Layout & Cuts (carpentry)

**What to record:**
* Cut list completed
* Accuracy ±1/16" count
* Rework count
* Date
* Verifier

**Purpose:** Shows measuring, layout, and cutting precision for carpentry work.

---

## Duct Seal (sheet metal)

**What to record:**
* Seam feet sealed
* Type used
* Leak spots re-sealed count
* Date
* Verifier

**Purpose:** Demonstrates proper sealing technique and quality checking.

---

## Seam Rolling (flooring)

**What to record:**
* Seam length
* Passes
* Defects corrected
* Date
* Verifier

**Purpose:** Shows flooring finishing technique and quality control.

---

## Housekeeping/Egress

**What to record:**
* Areas cleared
* Obstacles removed (count)
* Photos (no faces)
* Date
* Verifier

**Purpose:** Demonstrates commitment to site safety and cleanliness standards.

---

## Rigging Signals

**What to record:**
* Sessions count
* Signals practiced (list)
* Instructor initials
* Date

**Purpose:** Shows familiarity with standard rigging hand signals for safe communication.

---

**Notes:**

* All drills should be performed in a safe, supervised environment.
* Verifier should be an instructor, supervisor, or designated assessor.
* Photos must exclude faces and sensitive site identifiers.
* Records become proof artifacts for pathway packets and applications.
