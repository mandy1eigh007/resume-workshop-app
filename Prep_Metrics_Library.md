# Prep Metrics Library

> Named drills and how they're recorded

## Timed Carry (2×50 lb, 50 ft)

**Record:** time (mm:ss), path clear, PPE used, spotter Y/N, date, verifier initials.

## Conduit Bending (three offsets, 10°/22.5°/45°)

**Record:** angles measured (±°), take-up recorded, rework count, date, verifier initials.

## Device Terminations (10 devices)

**Record:** torque verified Y/N, continuity pass/fail count, time per device, date, verifier.

## Ladder Setup (24 ft)

**Record:** 4:1 rule verified, footing check, tie-off Y/N, inspector initials, date.

## Leak/Pressure (training rig)

**Record:** pressure target, duration, pass/fail, gauge photo (no faces), date, verifier initials.

## Layout & Cuts (carpentry)

**Record:** cut list completed, accuracy ±1/16" count, rework count, date, verifier.

## Duct Seal (sheet metal)

**Record:** seam feet sealed, type used, leak spots re-sealed count, date, verifier.

## Seam Rolling (flooring)

**Record:** seam length, passes, defects corrected, date, verifier.

## Housekeeping/Egress

**Record:** areas cleared, obstacles removed, photos (no faces), date, verifier.

## Rigging Signals

**Record:** sessions count, signals practiced, instructor initials, date.
