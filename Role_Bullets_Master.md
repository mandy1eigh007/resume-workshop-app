# Role → Bullet Bank

> Duty bullets per role; ≤24 words; measured/evidence-ready

## Line Cook

* Prepped, portioned, and labeled 60–90 covers/shift; maintained FIFO logs and line temps.
* Sanitized stations per checklist; logged times and chemical ratios.
* Staged deliveries (50–300 lb) with counts and rotation.
* Communicated tickets; recorded ticket-to-plate timing.

## Server

* Managed 8–12 table section; tracked orders, comps, and timing.
* Closed out drawer within $2 variance; reconciled logs.
* Coordinated runners/expo; paced service during peak.
* Upsold specials; recorded sales mix.

## Retail Associate

* Stocked 40–80 SKUs/shift; verified counts and shelf tags.
* Processed returns/exchanges; documented reasons.
* Organized backroom and maintained safety aisles (36").
* Assisted 30–60 customers; logged outcomes.

## Warehouse Associate

* Picked 80–200 lines/shift; recorded pick rates.
* Loaded pallets to pattern; shrink-wrapped and labeled.
* Scanned inventory and reconciled variances.
* Operated pallet jack safely; inspection checklist.

## Material Handler

* Moved 1–3 tons/day with carts/jacks; maintained egress.
* Staged by trade and sequence; counts verified.
* Labeled and protected materials; photo logs (no faces).
* Assisted with lift plans; exclusion zone control.

## Traffic Control / Flagger

* Set signs per plan; documented distances and timings.
* Maintained radio checks; communication logs.
* Monitored sightlines; adjusted setup; notes kept.
* Retrieved equipment; verified inventory.

## Janitor/Custodian

* Cleaned 20k–60k sq ft; checklist sign-offs.
* Maintained chemical mix logs; PPE recorded.
* Responded to spills; documented times.
* Inspected and restocked supplies.

## Security Guard

* Patrolled 4–8 zones/shift; logged incidents/time.
* Controlled access per list; badges checked.
* Monitored cameras; reported anomalies.
* Wrote shift reports; accurate times/locations.

## Landscaper/Groundskeeper

* Mowed/trimmed 10–20k sq ft; equipment checks.
* Pruned and removed debris; load counts.
* Irrigation checks; recorded run times.
* Safe tool handling; PPE logs.

## Demolition Laborer

* Removed finishes; tracked debris volume/weight.
* Protected egress and adjacent work; checklist.
* Operated saws with dust control; logs.
* Sorted recyclables; weight tickets.

## Carpenter Helper

* Staged lumber/hardware; counts and labels.
* Assisted cuts within ±1/16"; blade logs.
* Installed blocking/hardware; verified spacing.
* Cleanup and protection per plan.

## Drywall/Lather Helper

* Staged sheets; recorded counts and sizes.
* Assisted hangs; screw pattern checks.
* Cut openings to tolerance; waste tracked.
* Cleanup and protection maintained.

## Flooring Helper

* Prepped substrate; moisture readings logged.
* Staged materials; area and pattern notes.
* Assisted cuts and rolls; seam checks.
* Cleanup and VOC notes.

## Concrete Laborer

* Placed/rebar assistance; recorded placements.
* Screed/float support; timing logs.
* Curing protection installed; covered area.
* Cleanup and jointing checks.

## HVAC Helper

* Staged duct/fittings; counts verified.
* Assisted hangers; spacing logs.
* Sealed joints; sealant type/feet tracked.
* Labeled airflow and equipment.

## Electrical Helper

* Pulled cable; footage and labels recorded.
* Bent basic offsets; angle logs.
* Terminated devices; torque/checklist notes.
* Panel directory updates.

## Plumbing Helper

* Staged pipe/valves; counts/tags.
* Measured/cut/deburred; lengths logged.
* Hang/support installs; spacing checks.
* Pressure/leak test assistance; records.

## Sheet Metal Helper

* Duct install assistance; leak checks logged.
* Sealed and taped joints; material usage tracked.
* Hanger checks and labels.
* Clean and protect adjacent areas.

## Ironworker Helper

* Bolt/stage hardware; counts and tags.
* Maintain rigging inspections; dates recorded.
* Exclusion zones and housekeeping.
* Decking fastener counts.

## Roofer Helper

* Tear-off and staging; area logged.
* Underlayment install; overlaps recorded.
* Shingle/fastener pattern checks.
* Cleanup and protection.
